# Data Career Readiness: An Analysis of the U.S. Data Job Market

## Project Overview

As a recent Informatics graduate pursuing a career in data, I wanted to better understand today's job market through data itself. This project analyzes thousands of U.S. job postings and salary records to identify the skills employers seek, where opportunities are concentrated, and how salaries vary across different data careers.

## Research Question

What skills, locations, companies, and salary trends should aspiring data professionals understand when entering the U.S. job market?
